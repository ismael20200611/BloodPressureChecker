About Us

Welcome to the Blood Pressure Checker, your go-to tool for monitoring and understanding your blood pressure levels. Our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge and tools they need to manage their health effectively.

Academic Reference & Relevance

This project was designed to align with global standards in digital health monitoring. The logic and structure follow widely accepted medical thresholds recommended by institutions such as the World Health Organization (WHO) and the American Heart Association (AHA). This system may be suitable for educational demonstration, health tech prototypes, or research dissemination in digital health informatics, including conferences such as IEEE EMBS and JMIR.

Features

  • Easy-to-use interface for quick blood pressure checks
  • Real-time monitoring and historical data tracking
  • Customizable alerts for abnormal readings
  • Educational resources on blood pressure management
  • Secure data storage and privacy protection
